How It Works

Three Steps to Smart Tolling

From camera frame to carbon-adjusted toll in under 200ms — powered by computer vision and AI.

01
📷

Detect & Identify

Computer vision cameras capture every vehicle entering the toll zone. YOLO-based detection classifies vehicle type and estimates speed in real-time.

  • 30fps multi-lane camera stream
  • Instant plate anonymisation
  • Multi-vehicle simultaneous tracking
02
🧠

Analyse & Estimate

The AI engine estimates emission profiles based on vehicle class, engine type, behavioural patterns, and environmental context.

  • Carbon footprint scoring per vehicle
  • Behaviour & speed profiling
  • 85% confidence threshold check
03
💰

Price & Charge

Dynamic toll price calculated using base rate, carbon multiplier, emission index, and fairness adjustments — applied within 200ms.

  • Carbon multiplier dynamic pricing
  • Survival vehicle exemptions
  • Full transparent audit trail
Privacy First

Your Identity is Never Exposed

EDAT processes vehicle data without ever storing or transmitting a real licence plate. Every plate is transformed into a one-way SHA-256 cryptographic hash — anonymous, irreversible, and PDPA-compliant.

1

Camera Captures Plate

OCR reads "WXY 1234" from the licence plate region in real-time

2

Hashed On Edge Device

SHA-256 applied locally on the camera unit — raw plate never leaves the device

3

Only Hash Transmitted

A 64-char hex string is all we receive — a3f9c2e1... — mathematically irreversible
