Privacy First

Your Identity is Never Exposed

EDAT processes toll data with strict privacy layers. Incoming transactions are matched to registered RFID/payments. Unmatched data remains purely anonymous, while matched data is securely attached only to your hashed vehicle ID.

1

Anonymous Behaviour Layer

We track travel frequency, peak/off-peak times, and route patterns using only the hashed ID. AI analytics store only anonymised data.

2

Strict Identity Separation

Your account and vehicle info are isolated in an Identity Layer, completely inaccessible by AI processing and analytics.

3

Secure Mapping Logic

There is no continuous linkage between your identity and behaviour. Secure mapping occurs exclusively during rebate distribution.
