**Project: Eco-Dynamic AI Tolling (EDAT)**

*Constraint: Maximum 15 Slides*

## Slide 1: Title Slide
- **Title:** Eco-Dynamic AI Tolling (EDAT)
- **Subtitle:** Eradicating Congestion & Driving Net Zero with Agentic Infrastructure
- **Team Name & Track:** Track 4 (Green Horizon)

## Slide 2: The Core Problem (National Context)
- Severe mobility congestion in the Johor-Singapore Innovation Corridor.
- Static tolling systems fail to deter peak-hour traffic.
- Road infrastructure degrades rapidly under heavy loads during severe weather.

## Slide 3: The Missing Link in Net Zero
- We lack real-time financial incentives for commuters to shift behavior (off-peak, EV, public transit).
- Existing data is siloed: Transport ministries and environment agencies don't talk to each other in real-time.

## Slide 4: The Solution (EDAT)
- A dynamic, multi-modal pricing engine acting as the "intelligent brain" for highway networks.
- **Value Prop:** Charges fluctuate instantly based on real-time physics (Traffic Volume, Rain, Heat Stress) and time of day.

## Slide 5: The "Build With AI" Architecture (The Mandate)
- **Intelligence:** Gemini 1.5 Flash for rapid, multi-factor multiplier calculations and "Eco-Tip" generation.
- **Orchestration:** Firebase Genkit to coordinate the `AnalystAgent` and `LegalAgent`.
- **Context (RAG):** Vertex AI Search grounds the final toll cap against official Malaysian regulatory PDFs.

## Slide 6: How It Works: The Smart Dashboard
- (Screenshot of UI)
- Users see a direct comparison: "Drive Now (RM 9.50) vs. Take Public Transit (RM 3.40)".
- Gemini acts as a "Smart Advisor", generating a real-time tip (e.g., "Wait 30 mins to avoid the RM 2.00 Storm Surcharge!").

## Slide 7: Tech Stack & Cloud Deployment
- **Frontend:** Vanilla JS, CSS3, Leaflet, Google Routes.
- **Backend:** Node.js, Firebase Admin (Firestore for ESG trip logging).
- **Deployment:** Fully serverless on Google Cloud Run.
- **APIs:** MET Malaysia (Weather), data.gov.my (GTFS Transit Fares).

## Slide 9: Impact: Environmental (E)
- Smoothes out traffic peaks, reducing idling emissions.
- Directly incentivizes the adoption of Electric Vehicles (EVs) through instant toll discounts.

## Slide 10: Impact: Social & Commuter (S)
- Lowers the financial burden for flexible commuters.
- Weather-based pricing naturally deters non-essential travel during dangerous storms (reducing accidents).

## Slide 11: Impact: Governance & Infrastructure (G)
- Provides auditable, structured data grids for corporate ESG reporting.
- Saves highway operators millions by deterring heavy loads during extreme heat (asphalt stress).

## Slide 12: Business Model (B2G SaaS)
- **Revenue 1:** IaaS Licensing to highway operators (PLUS, EDL) - taking a micro-percentage of optimized toll yields.
- **Value:** The surplus revenue generated during critical peaks can cross-subsidize public transit infrastructure.

## Slide 13: Business Model (Corporate & Ads)
- **Revenue 2:** Corporate ESG Fleet Subscriptions for Scope 3 emissions tracking.
- **Revenue 3:** Sponsored Eco-Reward Ecosystem (Brands fund the Voucher Wallet to acquire eco-conscious users).

## Slide 14: Future Roadmap (Post-Hackathon)
- Integrate Computer Vision (YOLOv8) at physical toll booths for weight/axle detection.
- Expand multi-modal integration to cover RTS Link and ETS railways.
